Alina Baraz and the Galamatias

Alina Baraz and the Galamatias is an ecclectic band whose rythmic baselines and soothing melodies are inspired by the urban core.
Alina Baraz is the singer of the group while the Galamatias lend a supportive hand with instrumentals; focusing moreso on snares, snaps and synthesizers than on any particular instrument
